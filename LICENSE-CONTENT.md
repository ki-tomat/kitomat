## Dataset and Source Rules

KItomat content may include:

- synthetic example data
- self-created templates and checklists
- source lists with retrieval dates
- short original summaries with source references
- links to public primary or official sources

KItomat does not re-license third-party sources. External sources keep their own license terms. If origin or license is unclear, the material must not be merged into a release candidate.
