# Legal Boundaries

KItomat artifacts are working and orientation aids. They are not legal advice, audits or productive decision tools.

## No legal advice

Artifacts must not claim:

- legal correctness
- compliance approval
- audit completion
- automated eligibility decisions
- medical, legal, financial or HR final decisions

## Sensitive areas

Use extra caution with:

- compliance
- data protection
- EU AI Act proximity
- HR and recruiting
- finance and insurance
- health
- education opportunities
- legal assessments

For these areas:

- `human_review_required: true`
- at least `data_risk: yellow`
- official or primary sources required
- retrieval date required
- clear disclaimer required
- max `bronze_candidate` until trust review is complete
